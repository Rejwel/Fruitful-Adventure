## Fruitful Adventure V. 1.1

Welcome to our tower defense, in this game we are defending our farm family buildings from candy monsters!

Basic controls:
- **W,A,S,D** - Movement
- **G** - Grenade
- **TAB** - Equipment
- **Scroll or 1-5** - Weapons
